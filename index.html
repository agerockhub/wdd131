Accra, Ghana

Ghana is a West African country located along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean. Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and political stability, Ghana is home to diverse ethnic groups, including the Akan, Ewe, and Mole-Dagbani. The people are known for their warmth, hospitality, and strong sense of community. Traditional festivals, colorful attire, and high respect for elders reflect their way of life. Ghana blends modern living with deep-rooted customs, making it uniquely dynamic and culturally rich.
