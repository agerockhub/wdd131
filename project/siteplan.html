Site Name:Beyond The Cities

This is a captivating domain name that reflects the heart of this tourism initiative, inviting Ghanaians to discover the hidden gems that lie beyond urban life.

Purpose:for Beyond The Cities

by showcasing these destinations, we aim to foster a sense of national pride, stimulate the local economy through increased tourism, and support communities surrounding these sites. Ultimately, this initiative encourages Ghanaians to rediscover Ghana, embrace its heritage, and actively participate in preserving and enjoying the nation's diverse tourist offerings.

Color schema:

  • Primary Color: Forest Green (#2E7D32)
    • Meaning: Symbolizes nature, growth, and exploration.
    • Usage: Main buttons, headers, highlights.
  • Accent Color: Goldenrod (#FFB300)
    • Meaning: Represents sunshine, tradition, vibrancy.
    • Usage: Calls-to-action, icons, hover effects.
  • Secondary Color: Earth Brown (#6D4C41)
    • Meaning:Reflects soil, heritage, and groundedness.
    • Usage: Footer background, borders, text highlights.
  • Soft Background Color: Sand Beige (#FFF8E1)
    • Meaning: Warm, clean, and welcoming like natural light.
    • Usage: General background, content areas.
  • Contrast & Text Color: Charcoal Gray (#333333)
    • Meaning: Professional, easy to read, and modern.
    • Usage: Body text, headings, navigation.

Typography:

  • 1. Montserrat (for headings and navigation)
    • Why: Bold, clean, and professional with a contemporary look that grabs attention.
    • For:Page titles, section headers, navbar links.
    • Pairing style: Pairs well with softer body fonts like Open Sans or Lora.
  • 2. Lora (for body text)
    • Why: A serif font with a warm, elegant touch—great for long reads and storytelling.
    • For: Paragraphs, article content, descriptions.
    • Pairing style: Adds a graceful, traditional vibe that complements nature and culture themes.
  • 3. Roboto Condensed (for buttons, captions, and subheadings)
    • Why: Compact, easy to read, and great for UI elements where space is limited.
    • For: Button text, photo captions, cards, tour summaries, or pricing labels.
